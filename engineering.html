Summary of Relevant Experience:

During my undergraduate program, I gained experience working in the student Machine Shop as a Manager and Supervisor. This gave me a familiarty with common manufacturing processes and experience with relevant tools (mills, lathes, welders). Additonally, I worked with a team to design an ergonomic solution in collaboration with General Electric. I was the primary prototype designer, utilizing 3D printers to make the initial versions and then machining the final version.

After graduating, I started working with Aerospace Automation Equipment under Electroimpact at Boeing. To improve the equipment reliability, I designed various retrofits primarily for 3D printing and also collaborated with engineering teams for more complex assemblies.

In moving to the NYC area, I began working with Medical Device fixture design under Stryker. I primarily work with programming CMMs and designing simple yet robust fixtures while collaborating with an advanced engineering team.
